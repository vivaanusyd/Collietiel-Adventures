Six oysters for forty-two dollars :cockatiel-shocked: and they arrived on a
tray roughly the size of a coaster. Once I got past the bill, though, the

cold, shucked without a single shell fragment.

Service was sharp for how busy it was, and nobody hurried us off the table
:collie-sleepy:. The mignonette was doing very little :cockatiel-unimpressed:,
